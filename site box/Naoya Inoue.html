Naoya Inoue

Career, Achievements, and Titles

Biography

Naoya Inoue, born on April 10, 1993, in Zama, Kanagawa, Japan, is a Japanese professional boxer. Known as 'The Monster', Inoue is celebrated for his incredible power, speed, and technical prowess. He is regarded as one of the best pound-for-pound fighters in the world.

Titles and Belts

  • WBC Light-Flyweight World Champion
  • WBO Junior-Bantamweight World Champion
  • WBA (Super) Bantamweight World Champion
  • IBF Bantamweight World Champion

Key Career Highlights

  • Won the WBC Light-Flyweight Title in 2014
  • Captured the WBO Junior-Bantamweight Title in 2014
  • Dominated the Bantamweight division, winning the WBA and IBF titles
  • Winner of the 2019 World Boxing Super Series Bantamweight Tournament
  • Known for his devastating knockouts and unbeaten record
