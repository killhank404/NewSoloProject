Gary Russell Jr.

Career, Achievements, and Titles

Biography

Gary Allen Russell Jr., born on June 5, 1988, in Washington, D.C., USA, is an American professional boxer. Known for his exceptional hand speed and technical skills, Russell has made a significant impact in the featherweight division.

Titles and Belts

  • WBC Featherweight World Champion

Key Career Highlights

  • Won the WBC Featherweight Title in 2015
  • Successfully defended the WBC Featherweight Title multiple times
  • Notable victories over Jhonny Gonzalez, Patrick Hyland, and Joseph Diaz
  • Known for his speed and precision in the ring
