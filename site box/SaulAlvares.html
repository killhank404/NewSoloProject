Saul "Canelo" Alvarez

Career, Achievements, and Titles

Biography

Saul "Canelo" Alvarez, born on July 18, 1990, in Guadalajara, Jalisco, Mexico, is a Mexican professional boxer. Known for his exceptional skill, power, and head movement, Canelo is widely regarded as one of the best pound-for-pound fighters in the world.

Titles and Belts

  • WBC Light Middleweight World Champion
  • WBA (Super) Light Middleweight World Champion
  • WBC Middleweight World Champion
  • WBA (Super) Middleweight World Champion
  • WBC Super Middleweight World Champion
  • WBA (Super) Super Middleweight World Champion
  • IBF Middleweight World Champion
  • WBO Light Heavyweight World Champion

Key Career Highlights

  • Turned professional at the age of 15 in 2005
  • Defeated Shane Mosley, Austin Trout, and Erislandy Lara to solidify his position as a top fighter
  • Fought Gennady Golovkin twice, earning a draw and a victory in two highly anticipated bouts
  • Moved up to light heavyweight to defeat Sergey Kovalev and capture the WBO title
  • Unified the super middleweight division by defeating Caleb Plant in 2021
