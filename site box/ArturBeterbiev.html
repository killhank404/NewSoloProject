Artur Beterbiev

Career, Achievements, and Titles

Biography

Artur Beterbiev was born on January 21, 1985, in Khasavyurt, Russia. He started his boxing career at a young age and achieved significant success as an amateur, winning several national and international titles before turning professional.

Titles and Belts

  • Unified Light-Heavyweight World Champion (WBC, IBF, WBO)

Key Career Highlights

  • Gold Medal at the European Amateur Boxing Championships in 2006 and 2010
  • Turned professional in 2013
  • Won the IBF light-heavyweight title in 2017
  • Unified the WBC and IBF light-heavyweight titles in 2019
  • Defeated Joe Smith Jr. in 2022 to unify the WBC, IBF, and WBO titles
